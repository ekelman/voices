Q.   What is Bohire?

Here’s what it costs:
- Post your job openings for FREE.
- Choose the fee you want to pay.
- Bohire does not accept payment until you make a hire.
Here’s a small sample of what you get:
- Access to thousands of passive and active job seekers.
- A source that utilizes a time tested and highly successful recruiting method: the referral.
- A comprehensive and interactive Top Dawg Referrer Rating System™.
- The ability to control the message you present to potential candidates.

Q.   What does it mean to be an Employer?
Employers save time and money, and recruit quality employees for their jobs. Despite paying significantly less money than other traditional recruiting sources, Bohire satisfies Employers by providing quality candidates for these positions. Employers can post jobs for FREE and have the ability to select a payment as low as 1% of first year salary.

Q.   What is a Cash Referral Reward?
The Cash Referral Reward is the amount of money the Employer pays the Referrer for finding the right candidate for their job posting. The Employer sets the amount of the Referral Reward. The Employer only pays AFTER a successful hire has been made.
Top
 
Q.   How much should I offer as a Reward?
The Referral Reward is set by the Employer. For Full Time positions the Employer can select a fee from 1 to 30% of first year salary. If the position is Part Time or Temporary, the employer can set a flat rate Referral Reward.
When choosing your Cash Referral Reward, consider these factors:
Is your Referral Reward competitive?
Is your Referral Reward significant enough to motivate the Referrers to refer quality candidates? We recommend a fee between 5 to 8% of first year salary.
Is this a hard-to-fill position, do you need someone urgently, are you recruiting in a competitive environment? If so, consider raising the Referral Reward to motivate more people to refer quality candidates to your position.

Q.   Why does Bohire use a Top Dawg Referral Rating System™?
Bohire’s Top Dawg Referrer Rating System was created to ensure the Bohire community is made up of highly qualified Referrers. Employers are given the ability to rate every Referrer on their ability to refer quality candidates. We know that recruiting quality employees is a time consuming and costly process and knowing the quality of the source is the first step to finding your next great employee. Referrers are rated on a scale of one to five with five being the highest rating.

Q.   How do I rate a Referrer?
Rating a Referrer is extremely easy and only takes a few seconds. A Referrer should be rated on the quality of the candidate that he/she submits to your job posting. If the candidate was a great match for the job, the Referrer should be rewarded with a great rating. Conversely, if the candidate was not at all suitable for the position the Referrer should receive a low rating. Your decision to hire/not hire the candidate should not have any bearing on the rating, what is important is the candidate’s suitability for the position.
Top
 
Q.   Why is it important to rate Referrers?
It is extremely important to rate every Referrer and to rate them fairly and truthfully. A fair and accurate rating will be very useful to yourself and your fellow members and as ratings accumulate it will serve as an invaluable tool in the selection process. Great referrers will refer great candidates and our Top Dawg Referral Rating System™ will allow you to distinguish the great Referrers from the poor ones.

Q.   What does it mean to be a Referrer?
If you know great people, and many of us do, then you have the ability to be a great Referrer. A Referrer’s main responsibility is to understand the needs of friends who are interested in job opportunities. You must understand what your friends are looking for in a job, a career, and a company and be able to match those needs to job openings. Referrers review the job descriptions and submit suitable candidates to employers. If your friend is hired, you receive the “Cash Referral Reward”.

Q.   How does it work?
Sign up to be a Bohire Referrer. It’s free and only takes a few minutes.
Search Job Postings and match a job with a friend.
Send your friend (the Candidate) the job posting. If they're interested, a Candidate Profile is created. This is a summary of your friend’s skills, education and experience.
Send your friend’s Candidate Profile to the Employer. The Referrer’s work is now done.
If the Employer is interested in your Candidate, they will initiate the interview with your friend.
If the Candidate is successful in getting hired, the Referrer is paid the “Cash Referral Reward”.
Everyone wins at Bohire!

Q.   How does the Top Dawg Referral Rating System™ work?
Every Referrer will be rated on their ability to match a friend to a job posting.  Every time you submit a friend to an employer, this employer will rate your friend’s suitability for that job.  If this friend’s skills, education and experience match the demands of the position, your candidate will be considered a good match and you will receive a favourable rating.  Your rating will be a direct reflection on your ability to refer quality candidates to open postings.

Q.   How am I notified of job openings?
When you join the Bohire Community as a Referrer you will be asked to select categories that you wish to be notified about when new job openings are posted.  When a new job posting matches one of your selected categories you will be notified through email.  The categories you select should contain job postings that your candidates are interested in.  Please remember to keep your selections current.  You also have the ability to modify your selections in your Edit My Profile section. 
When you search job postings you also have the ability to use these selections to customize your search.  By selecting the My Preferences search feature you will be searching only open postings in the job categories you selected.

Q.   How do I keep track of my friends who are interested in job opportunities?

You can keep track of your candidates in the My Friends section.  Once a friend accepts your email invitation (the email must be sent through the Bohire website) he/she will be recorded as a Candidate in your My Friends list.  Once a friend is referred to a job posting, he/she will be recorded in the My Friends Referred to Open Postings list.  You can track each friend’s progress through the interview process using this list.   The employer updates the status bar to inform you at what stage your candidate is at in the interview process.

Q.   Who should I use as a Referrer?
There are several factors that make a great Referrer.  Before selecting a Referrer here are a few suggestions:
Select someone who is responsible, trustworthy and self motivated. 
Select someone who has a strong reputation in the industry you want to work in.
Select someone that understands your personal and professional needs.
 
Top
 
Q.   Can I use more than one Referrer?
You can use more than one Referrer, however if you select a Referrer that fits the criteria above, there may not be a need to use anyone else.  It is not recommended to use any more than two Referrers.  If you are working with two Referrers and wish to work with an alternate Referrer you may do so at anytime, however you will need to ask your existing Referrer to delete your profile.  If this process is unsuccessful please submit your name and your Referrer’s name to deletecandidate@bohire.com and our administrator will delete your profile.   
Top
 
Q.   What information should I provide to my Referrer?
The more information you supply to your Referrer, the better your chances are of being hired. Depending on the stage of your job search here are some suggestions on the information you should provide:
A Referrer you trust has invited you to join his/her network and you accept. Your name and email address are then recorded in the Referrer’s My Friends Looking for Jobs list.
You are then asked to complete a Candidate Profile, which you can complete immediately or at a later date (for example, when a suitable opportunity is found).
At this point your Referrer will begin searching the Bohire website for suitable opportunities, however the more information he/she has about you the better their chances are of finding the right job opening.
You are interested in a job and would like your Referrer to submit your profile. At this point you are required to provide, at a minimum, a complete Candidate Profile. You can also supply a resume and any additional supporting documents.
