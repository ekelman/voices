About us
Whether you’re looking for a great job or hiring a great employee, both tasks can be challenging and time consuming.  Bohire is a new and exciting alternative that helps the job seeker and employer overcome these obstacles. 

Bohire believes the employment referral is the best method of recruiting.  Most of us know great people and have even recommended friends for jobs.  Studies have shown that hires made through a referral yield quality candidates that have higher job satisfaction and higher retention rates.  When a successful match is made, the employer gets a great employee and the friend gets a great job.  Everybody wins!

However, referring great employees is limited in scope because people only know so many employers and employers can only ask for referrals from so many people.  Bohire is taking this time tested, highly successful recruiting source to the next level. 

Bohire is Canada’s Leading Job Referral Community.  Employers post their jobs on the Bohire website for FREE! Members of the Bohire Referral Community know great people who are interested in great jobs.  Referrers view the job postings, refer great people and hires are made.  The employer offers a CASH REWARD to the Referrers for these referrals.

The TMQ Advantage
 
 
In today’s market, recruiting top talent is highly competitive.  Employers need to consider many factors when eloping their recruitment strategies and at Bohire, we believe we’ve created recruiting solution that addresses your three most prevalent needs:  
 
1) Time 

Bohire realizes employers receive hundreds of resumes through high volume sources like newspaper ads and job boards, and screening these resumes is a time consuming process.  Posting a job on the Bohire website will yield quality candidates instead of quantities of them because each applicant is referred to your posting by a credible source.  Secondly the Bohire community is made up of thousands of Referrers and as a result you have the ability to cast an extremely wide net.  Casting a wide net over a community of credible Referrers that understand your job posting will equate to timely, quality hires.   
 
2) Money

Simply put, Bohire is the most employer friendly recruitment solution on the market today. Employers can post their jobs on the Bohire website for FREE.  You are only required to make a payment when a hire is made and you are in complete control of your costs because you decide what fee you want to pay.    
 
3) Quality 

We know your time is valuable and as such it is extremely important that you work with Referrers that understand your needs and are committed to making quality matches.  Our community of Referrers are targeted professionals, meaning candidates that are presented to employers come from credible, knowledgeable sources! In addition, Bohire’s Referrer Rating System allows employers to easily measure the performance and ability of every Referrer.
